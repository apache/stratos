WSO2 Stratos Services Sign In

You can use this form to sign in to the WSO2 Stratos Admin Console.

The common login: When accessed through https://stratoslive.wso2.com

Figure1: Sign In Form When accessed through https://stratoslive.wso2.com

The Form Field Description
Username

For this field you need to enter the username in the following format.

yourname@yourdomain

E.g. foo@wso2.com

Please note, your username will be different from your email address (unless you chose the domain name as the domain of your email provider, and the admin name as same as your email user name, at the time of registering your account).

Your email address can't be used interchangeably with the username, to log in.
Password The Password of your user account
